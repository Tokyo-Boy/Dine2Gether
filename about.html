About Dine2Gether

Our Mission

Founded with a simple yet powerful belief: nobody should have to eat alone unless they want to. Food brings people together, but modern, busy schedules often turn breakfast, lunch, and dinner into isolated routines.

Dine2Gether was created to build bridges. Whether you are stopping by a neighborhood family diner for comfort food, checking out a brand new bistro, or making a quick late-night run for burgers and fries, our platform safely connects you with real local people ready to join you at the table.

We take pride in building an inclusive community. Our design principles guarantee an accessible layout with clear text and straightforward operations, making it easy for users of all abilities to find companionship over a great meal.

Our Leadership

Julian Vance

Founder & CEO

Julian started Dine2Gether after moving to a new city and realizing how difficult it was to find people to check out local food spots with. Dedicated to accessible design and community building, he believes that the best conversations happen when sharing a meal.
